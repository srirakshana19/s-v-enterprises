About Us

Engineering Excellence Built on Decades of Experience

S V Enterprises is a trusted lift installation and engineering solutions company. Over the past 15 years, we have delivered reliable elevator installations, modernization plans, and strict maintenance protocols for residential, commercial, and industrial developments throughout India.

Our leadership brings hands-on experience in executing large-scale projects alongside globally recognized manufacturers including Fujitec, OTIS, KONE, Hitachi, Schindler, Johnson Lifts, TK Elevator, and other leading brands.

Specialized High-Rise Teams: Executing high-speed installations with exact tolerances.

Our Services

From precision lift installation to advanced automatic parking systems, we deliver end-to-end elevator engineering.

Lift Installation

Professional installation of passenger, service, and freight elevators for residential complexes, commercial spaces, and industrial towers.

Elevator Modernization

Upgrading older elevator setups with contemporary controller tech, gear systems, and aesthetic cabin designs for better safety and lower energy usage.

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Scheduled preventive maintenance, thorough lubrication checks, safety testing, and proactive parts replacement to prevent unexpected breakdowns.

Breakdown & Repair Services

Prompt on-demand troubleshooting and high-quality repairs with minimal downtime to restore elevator functionality quickly and safely.

High-Rise Elevator Installation

Specialized vertical alignment, rope suspension, and testing for high-rise towers, utilizing state-of-the-art hoisting setups.

Automatic Car Parking Lift

Expert installation of modern automated multi-level car parking systems to optimize vertical space in commercial hubs.

Technical Consultancy

Expert pre-construction guidance covering shaft dimension planning, load requirements, traffic flow analysis, and brand comparisons.

Testing & Commissioning

Comprehensive pre-handover assessment measuring speed, ride quality, safety sensor trips, and overload mechanisms to guarantee safety.
